公司背景 COMPANY PROFILE

One of the largest, most sustainable
printing & packaging factories in Bangladesh.

Established in 2018, our plant sits in the green north countryside of Dhaka — around 60 minutes from Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport. Across 150,000 square meters we run a complete set of advanced elastic, printing and process equipment, serving local apparel factories and international garment brands with one promise in the name: good, and fast.

品質控制 QUALITY CONTROL

Quality is the lifeblood
of this factory.

Strict control runs from the first yarn to the final carton. Our own dyeing house and a cutting-edge internal laboratory — built to international standards — monitor colour fastness, tensile strength and chemical compliance on every order before it leaves the gate.

  • In-house, international-standard testing laboratory
  • Own dyeing house for tapes, yarns and threads
  • Heidelberg & KBA presses with full back-end finishing
  • Third-party audits passed for major global brands
